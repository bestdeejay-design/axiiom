Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a fintech platform?

A minimum viable payment platform typically takes 3–5 months. Full-featured platforms with multi-acquiring, escrow, and compliance integrations range from 6–12 months depending on complexity.

Do you handle PCI DSS certification?

Yes. We build PCI DSS Level 1 compliance into the architecture from day one and guide you through the SAQ/ROC process. Our infrastructure is pre-certified, which significantly reduces your audit timeline.

Can you integrate with existing banking systems?

We have pre-built integrations with major acquiring banks, core banking platforms, KYC/AML providers, and credit bureaus. Custom integrations are typically delivered within 2–4 weeks.

What's your engagement model?

We work on dedicated team, fixed-price, or build-operate-transfer models. All engagements include a discovery sprint, compliance scoping, and a clear architecture plan before development begins.
