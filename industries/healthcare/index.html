Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a healthcare platform?

A HIPAA-compliant MVP typically takes 3-5 months. Full EHR systems with FHIR integration, telemedicine, and analytics range from 6-12 months depending on complexity and interoperability requirements.

How do you ensure HIPAA compliance in development?

We build on AWS HIPAA-eligible infrastructure with BAAs in place. Every deployment includes encryption at rest and in transit, audit logging, automated access reviews, and annual penetration testing.

Can you integrate with existing EHR systems?

Yes. We have pre-built integrations with Epic, Cerner, Athenahealth, and Practice Fusion via FHIR APIs. Custom HL7 interfaces are typically delivered within 3-6 weeks.

What's your engagement model for healthcare projects?

We offer dedicated team, fixed-price, and build-operate-transfer models. Every engagement starts with a compliance scoping sprint to define the BAA, architecture, and regulatory requirements.
