Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a SIEM platform?

A working SIEM MVP with log aggregation and correlation rules typically takes 4-6 months. Full platforms with SOAR, UEBA, and threat intelligence range from 8-14 months.

How do you ensure threat detection accuracy?

We combine signature-based detection with ML anomaly models, reduce false positives through correlation rules, and continuously tune detection based on real-world feedback.

Can you build FedRAMP-compliant solutions?

Yes. We architect for FedRAMP Moderate and High baselines, implement continuous monitoring, and automate evidence collection for the authorization package.

What's your engagement model for security projects?

We offer dedicated team, fixed-price, and build-operate-transfer models. Every engagement starts with threat modeling and a security architecture review.
