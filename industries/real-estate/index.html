Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a real estate platform?

A custom CRM or MLS platform typically takes 3-5 months for an MVP. Full-featured platforms with transaction management, IDX, and integrations range from 6-10 months depending on scope.

Can you integrate with existing MLS boards?

Yes. We have experience integrating with major MLS providers including MLS Listings, Bright MLS, CRMLS, and local boards via RETS, RESO Web API, and IDX feeds.

How do you handle IDX compliance?

We build IDX/IXL solutions that comply with each MLS board's rules, including data display requirements, update schedules, opt-out policies, and branding guidelines.

What's your engagement model for real estate projects?

We offer dedicated team, fixed-price, and build-operate-transfer models. Each engagement starts with a workflow discovery sprint to map agent processes and compliance requirements.
